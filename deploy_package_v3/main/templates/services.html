Holistic Care & Support

A Complete Support System

At Holistic Foster Care Foundation, we provide comprehensive physical, emotional, and social support for orphaned and underprivileged girls in Lahore.

Our holistic approach ensures that each girl receives:

  • Safe and nurturing accommodation
  • Emotional support and counseling
  • Community integration activities
  • Personalized care plans
Our Commitment

Founded by Batool Bibi, a compassionate nurse from the UK, our foundation is dedicated to providing a safe haven where girls can thrive in all aspects of their lives. We believe that true care means addressing the whole person - mind, body, and spirit.

Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure each girl feels valued, supported, and empowered to build a brighter future.

Quality Education

Empowering Through Education

We believe education is the cornerstone of empowerment. Our foundation partners with local schools to provide formal education and comprehensive learning support.

Our educational program includes:

  • Access to quality schooling
  • After-school tutoring and homework help
  • Computer and digital literacy
  • Academic progress monitoring
Education That Transforms Lives

Our education program is designed to ensure that each girl has the support she needs to succeed academically. We work closely with local schools and educators to create personalized learning plans that address individual needs and goals.

With dedicated teachers like Fazeelat Qaiser and Tamkinat Qureshi, our girls receive quality instruction and mentorship both in and out of the classroom.

"Education is not just about academics; it's about giving these girls the tools they need to build independent futures and break the cycle of poverty."

Healthcare & Nutrition

Comprehensive Health & Wellbeing

With our founder's background as a healthcare professional, we place special emphasis on the physical health and nutrition of our girls.

Our healthcare program ensures:

  • Nutritionally balanced meals
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Dental and vision care
  • Mental health support
  • Physical fitness activities
Health as a Foundation for Success

Drawing on Batool Bibi's extensive experience as a nurse in the UK for over 20 years, our healthcare program is designed with the highest standards. We understand that good health is essential for learning, development, and overall wellbeing.

Our team includes qualified nutritionists like Tamkinat Qureshi who ensure that the girls receive well-balanced meals that support their growth and development.

Emotional Development

Building Emotional Resilience

Many of our girls have experienced trauma and loss. Our emotional development program helps them heal and build the confidence they need to thrive.

Key components include:

  • Professional counseling
  • Mentorship programs
  • Trust-building activities
  • Personal growth workshops
Expert-Led Emotional Support

Under the guidance of Nighat Yasmin, our CEO and a qualified psychologist, we implement specialized programs designed to help girls process their experiences, build resilience, and develop healthy emotional responses.

We recognize that emotional wellbeing is essential for academic success and future independence. Our approach combines individual counseling with group activities that foster connection and belonging.

Our Approach

We create a safe space where girls can express themselves, learn emotional regulation, and develop positive coping strategies. Through creative arts, storytelling, and guided reflection, we help them build the emotional intelligence they need to navigate life's challenges.

Connecting With the Broader World

Common questions about our foundation

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing home for orphaned and underprivileged girls in Lahore, Pakistan. We focus on their holistic development through quality education, healthcare, emotional support, and skill development, empowering them to build independent and fulfilling futures.

You can support our foundation by making a donation, sponsoring a girl's education or healthcare, volunteering your time and skills, or helping spread awareness about our work. Every contribution, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference in these girls' lives. Contact us directly to discuss how you can get involved.

Your donations directly support our girls' well-being and development. Funds are used for daily operational costs, school fees and educational materials, nutritious meals and healthcare, staff salaries, and recreational activities. We maintain transparency in our financial operations and are committed to maximizing the impact of every contribution.

The foundation was established by Batool Bibi, a dedicated nurse with over 20 years of experience in the UK healthcare system. After witnessing the struggles of orphaned and underprivileged girls in Pakistan, she left her career abroad to create a comprehensive support system that addresses not just their basic needs, but their complete well-being and future prospects.

We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. Whether you're a teacher, healthcare professional, artist, or simply passionate about helping, there are numerous ways to contribute. Contact us at +92 322 0711929 or visit our center at 6F-2 Model Town Link Road, Lahore to discuss volunteer opportunities that match your skills and availability.
