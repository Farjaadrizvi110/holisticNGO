faq

Common questions about our foundation

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing home for orphaned and underprivileged girls in Lahore, Pakistan. We focus on their holistic development through quality education, healthcare, emotional support, and skill development, empowering them to build independent and fulfilling futures.

You can support our foundation by making a donation, sponsoring a girl's education or healthcare, volunteering your time and skills, or helping spread awareness about our work. Every contribution, regardless of size, makes a meaningful difference in these girls' lives. Contact us directly to discuss how you can get involved.

Your donations directly support our girls' well-being and development. Funds are used for daily operational costs, school fees and educational materials, nutritious meals and healthcare, staff salaries, and recreational activities. We maintain transparency in our financial operations and are committed to maximizing the impact of every contribution.

The foundation was established by Batool Bibi, a dedicated nurse with over 20 years of experience in the UK healthcare system. After witnessing the struggles of orphaned and underprivileged girls in Pakistan, she left her career abroad to create a comprehensive support system that addresses not just their basic needs, but their complete well-being and future prospects.

We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. Whether you're a teacher, healthcare professional, artist, or simply passionate about helping, there are numerous ways to contribute. Contact us at +92 322 0711929 or visit our center at 6F-2 Model Town Link Road, Lahore to discuss volunteer opportunities that match your skills and availability.
