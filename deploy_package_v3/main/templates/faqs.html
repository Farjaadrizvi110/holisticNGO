General FAQs

Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing home for orphaned and underprivileged girls in Lahore, Pakistan. We focus on their holistic development through quality education, healthcare, emotional support, and skill development to help them become independent, confident women who can contribute positively to society.

There are several ways to get involved: you can make a donation to support our programs, sponsor a girl's education and care, volunteer your time and skills, or help spread awareness about our cause. Visit our "Get Involved" page or contact us directly at +92 322 0711929 to discuss how you can contribute to our mission.

Holistic Foster Care Foundation was founded by Batool Bibi, a compassionate nurse who left her career in the UK to establish this safe haven for girls in Pakistan. With over 20 years of healthcare experience, she was driven by her desire to give back to her homeland and address the challenges faced by orphaned and underprivileged girls in Lahore.

Our foundation is located at 6F-2 Model Town Link Road, Lahore, 54600, Pakistan. We chose Lahore as our location because it's a vibrant city with diverse communities, including many marginalized groups that need support. Our goal is to create a model that can eventually be replicated across Pakistan.

Unlike traditional orphanages that focus primarily on basic necessities, we take a comprehensive approach to child development through four key pillars: mental and emotional well-being, physical health, education and skill development, and social integration. Each girl is treated as an individual with unique potential, receiving personalized support to help her grow into a confident, independent woman.

Volunteer FAQs

We welcome volunteers with various skills and backgrounds. To apply, please contact us with information about your skills, availability, and how you'd like to contribute. We particularly need volunteers for tutoring, mentoring, organizing recreational activities, healthcare services, administrative support, and fundraising. All volunteers undergo a screening process to ensure the safety and well-being of our girls.

We have various volunteer opportunities including: educational tutors in subjects like English, mathematics, and science; mentors who can provide guidance and emotional support; healthcare professionals for check-ups and health education; IT and computer skills trainers; arts and crafts instructors; administrative assistants; and fundraising and event organizers. We try to match volunteers' skills with our needs for the most meaningful impact.

Yes, we offer remote volunteering opportunities for international supporters. You can contribute through virtual tutoring, mentoring via video calls, assisting with social media management, grant writing, fundraising campaigns, or providing professional expertise in areas like website development, graphic design, or marketing. Remote volunteers help us extend our reach and bring diverse perspectives to our organization.

Program FAQs

We provide comprehensive educational support including formal schooling through partnerships with local schools, after-school tutoring, homework assistance, and supplementary learning in subjects like English, mathematics, and science. Beyond academics, we offer skill development programs in computer literacy, arts and crafts, and life skills. Our educational approach is tailored to each girl's needs, abilities, and interests to maximize her potential.

Emotional well-being is a cornerstone of our approach. We provide regular counseling sessions with our in-house psychologist, Nighat Yasmin, who specializes in child psychology. We create a nurturing environment where girls feel safe to express themselves, and implement mentorship programs pairing girls with positive role models. Through group activities that build self-confidence and resilience, we help them process past traumas and develop healthy emotional regulation skills.

We are committed to supporting our girls through their transition to adulthood. Our programs include higher education guidance, vocational training partnerships, job placement assistance, and life skills training for independent living. We maintain a support network for alumni and provide transitional housing options when needed. Our goal is to ensure each young woman leaves our care with the confidence, skills, and resources to build a successful, independent life.
