Passionate Hearts Behind Holistic Foster Care Foundation

Meet the dedicated individuals who have committed their lives to creating a safe, nurturing environment for orphaned and underprivileged girls in Lahore, Pakistan. Our team combines healthcare expertise, educational experience, and boundless compassion.

Batool Bibi - Founder

Batool Bibi has been a dedicated nurse in the UK for over 20 years, providing compassionate care and service to her community. Beyond her professional work, she has made a significant impact through her philanthropic efforts, particularly in supporting underprivileged girls in Pakistan.

She established Holistic Foster Care Foundation, offering girls shelter, education, and a chance at a brighter future. Her mission is empowering girls by providing them with the necessary resources and opportunities to break the cycle of poverty and build independent lives.

Through her unwavering dedication, Batool Bibi continues to change lives and inspire others to contribute to meaningful social causes.

Nighat Yasmin - CEO

Nighat Yasmin is a dedicated educator, psychologist, and advocate for girls' well-being. With a strong background in teaching, she has been instrumental in supporting young girls through tailored well-being programs.

As the founder and operator of a school, she is committed to providing quality education and fostering a nurturing learning environment. In addition to her work in education, Nighat conducts specialized sessions with girls at Holistic Foster Care Foundation, offering psychological support to help them build resilience, confidence, and emotional well-being.

Her passion for empowering young minds continues to make a meaningful impact in their lives.

Twitter Facebook Instagram
Fazeelat Qaiser - Teacher

Fazeelat Qaiser is a dedicated and compassionate teacher with a strong commitment to supporting young girls in their educational and personal development. She plays a vital role at Holistic Foster Care Foundation, providing day-to-day care, homework assistance, and out-of-school educational activities to help the girls thrive.

With a nurturing approach, she ensures that each child receives the guidance and support they need to succeed academically and emotionally. Her dedication to creating a positive and supportive environment makes a lasting impact on the lives of the girls she mentors.

Twitter Facebook Instagram
Tamkinat Qureshi - English Teacher

Professionally, Tamkinat Qureshi is a qualified nutritionist, and pursues teaching as a fulfilling hobby. Over the years, she has had the privilege of volunteering with several NGOs and institutions, gaining invaluable experience in community service.

Tamkinat has chosen to remain with HFCF due to her desire to contribute to making a tangible difference. For her, HFCF has become like a second home. The individuals behind this foundation pour their hearts into their work, striving tirelessly to create positive change within the community.

"I see myself continuing to be an integral part of this remarkable foundation for many years to come, contributing to its mission and making meaningful improvements wherever possible."

Twitter Facebook Instagram
Hussain Suhail - Volunteer

Hussain Suhail is a dedicated social worker and the current administrator at Smart School in Model Town, Lahore. As a Pakistan national speaker for the Declamation Society, he actively engages in inspiring and empowering individuals through his speeches.

His vision for Holistic Foster Care Foundation Girls is to ensure their strong presence in the community, enabling them to become independent, support their families, and contribute to society. Committed to changing lives, Hussain Suhail believes in providing these girls with the necessary resources and opportunities to build a brighter future.

Through his work, he continues to advocate for education, empowerment, and social inclusion.

Shakil Salam - Global Support

Shakil's journey in supporting charity started when he met Batool Bibi who has been passionate about setting up a center for girls.

Shakil has moved to UK in 1981. Alongside his roles in public service, Shakil has been actively involved in community media.

Shakil Salam's multifaceted career underscores a profound dedication to journalism, public health, and community service and that is the reason he is keen to support Holistic Foster Care Foundation globally.

Dania Tanveer - Administrator

Dania, as the administrator at Holistic Foster Care Foundation, plays a vital role in ensuring the center runs smoothly and provides a safe, nurturing environment for the girls. With her background in Mass Communication from LCWU, she brings strong organizational and communication skills to the foundation.

Her passion for making a difference drives her to manage daily operations, support the staff, and create a positive impact on the lives of the girls. She is also passionate for exploring new things and places.

Farwa - Chef

Farwa works as the chef at Holistic Foster Care Foundation, where she plays a vital role in ensuring the girls receive nutritious and well-balanced meals. She carefully prepares and distributes food, making sure everyone is well-fed and taken care of.
